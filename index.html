Intro

Hi. I am Thomas Vuillaume. I currently hold a postdoctoral position at LAPP (CNRS) in Annecy (France). My research deals with Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN) emission (from observations to theory). I am a member of the HESS collaboration as well as the CTA consortium . I am also leading the data integration task under european mission ASTERICS .

Curriculum Rapidum

  • March - July 2012 : Internship at IPAG
  • 2012 : Master 2 Astrophysique, Plasmas, Planètes, Université Joseph Fourier, Grenoble.
  • 2012 : Diploma from engineering school Phelma, Grenoble. Specialization in Physics and Nanosciences.
  • June - August 2010 : Internship at the astrophysics laboratory of the University of Exeter in England
